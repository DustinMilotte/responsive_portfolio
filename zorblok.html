Riding With Zorblok

Bose AR (Unity)

In "Riding With Zorblok" your peaceful day of listening to music is hijacked by a being from another planet. He's holding your brain hostage until you help him get to a party and win back his girlfriend in this choose your own adventure style Bose AR audio experience. Winner of Best Audio AR at the Unity NYC XR Jam 2019. Join the TestFlight on iOS or contact me for a current Andriod APK.

Enhanced by Bose AR "Zorblok" lets the user guide the narrative by making decisions with physical gestures like yes and no, you will also have to steer his spaceship by turning your head! Bose AR allows for a "phone down, hands free" experience that allows the user to remain engaged in the real world.

YouTube video

The team that developed "Zorblok" has expanded the idea into AudVenture, a platform for user generated interactive audio stories. AudVenture will allow non-developers to create and share similar stories with a simple code-free node based user interface. From podcasters to dungeon masters, fans of interactive fictions and the visually impaired our goal is to create a community for interactive audio experiences.
